## Dataset Characteristics

1. Article Structure
   - Title
   - Full text content
   - Subject category
   - Publication date

2. Key Features
   - Balanced distribution between true and fake news
   - Diverse subject categories
   - Temporal coverage from 2016 to 2018
   - Various writing styles and sources

3. Subject Categories
   True News:
   - politicsNews
   - worldnews

   Fake News:
   - News
   - politics
   - left-news
   - Government News
   - US_News
   - Middle-east

4. Text Properties
   - Varying article lengths
   - Different writing styles
   - Mix of formal and informal language
   - Various topics and themes
